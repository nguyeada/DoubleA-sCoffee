Double A's Coffee is a small but growing chain with 10 locations across the US. This chain serves roughly 500 customers a day at each location for a total of 5,000 customers across all stores. In order to revamp its menu, the chain needs to be able to track the number of orders and different drinks sold at each location. This database will help each store analyze the popular drink orders for their area, allowing them to adjust their menu's accordingly.
